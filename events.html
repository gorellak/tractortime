Spring Pull and Swap Meet May 18th, 2019 Chippewa Valley Antique Engine and Model Club presents our annual Tractor Pull and Swap Meet. Gate admission is $2 per day, with kids 12 and under free.

For more information click on the show flyers or check out the tractor pull rules here.
56th Annual Pioneer Days August 9-11, 2019 Pioneer Days is a three day event where people come to compete in tractor pulls, show off thier antique tractors and equipment, and enjoy the history of farming pioneer life. With displays from threshing to blackmsmithing, there's something for everyone.

Gate Admission:
$7 per day per person Age 12 and under FREE Souvenir buttons available. Have something interesting and historic you want to display like an antique tractor? Become an exhibitor and get in free!

Primitive Camping (no electricity) available first come, first served $8 per day for every day parked on showgrounds

Handicap parking is available on the main showgrounds with People wagon tours around showgrounds.

Crafts and numerous other displays are around the grounds to enjoy.
